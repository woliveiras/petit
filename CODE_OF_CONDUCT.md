# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Petit is committed to a community that is open, welcoming, beginner-friendly, and inclusive.

We want everyone to feel safe participating in this project, regardless of age, body size, caste, disability, ethnicity, level of experience, education, family status, gender identity and expression, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual orientation, or socioeconomic status.

We value respectful collaboration, empathy, and learning. Questions from newcomers are welcome. Mentorship and constructive feedback are encouraged.

## Expected Behavior

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- Being friendly, patient, and respectful.
- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respecting different viewpoints, experiences, and technical backgrounds.
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully.
- Acknowledging mistakes, apologizing when needed, and learning from them.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and users.
- Helping beginners learn without ridicule, gatekeeping, or shaming.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, bullying, stalking, or threats in any form.
- Discriminatory, hateful, or exclusionary language, jokes, or imagery.
- Sexualized language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- Personal attacks, insults, trolling, or hostile conduct.
- Publishing private information (doxxing) without explicit permission.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions, reviews, or community spaces.
- Retaliation against anyone who reports a concern in good faith.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and review comments.
- Documentation and communication channels managed by the project.
- Public interactions where someone is representing Petit.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

1. For a confidential report, open a private GitHub Security Advisory in this repository and clearly mark it as "Code of Conduct Report".
2. If private advisory reporting is not possible, contact the maintainer through the private contact channel listed in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

When reporting, include:

- What happened.
- Links, screenshots, or any relevant evidence.
- Time, context, and who was involved (if known).

All reports will be reviewed promptly and fairly. We will protect reporter privacy to the extent possible.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Possible enforcement actions include:

1. Private warning and request to stop the behavior.
2. Temporary moderation actions (comment removal, conversation lock, temporary interaction limits).
3. Temporary suspension from community spaces.
4. Permanent ban from participation in project spaces.

Enforcement decisions will consider context, severity, and repeated behavior. Retaliation for reporting is itself a violation.

### Response Targets

- Acknowledgement: up to 3 business days.
- Initial review: up to 7 business days.
